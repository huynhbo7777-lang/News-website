Xung dot Trung Dong

Israel - Iran lien tuc an mieng tra mieng, so nguoi chet tang cao

Nguon: Thanh Nien 15/06/2025 5 phut doc

Bai bao mo ta cuc dien leo thang nhanh khi Israel va Iran lien tiep tien hanh nhung dot tan cong dap tra, lam so thuong vong tang cao o ca hai phia. Israel canh bao nguoi dan ve nhung ngay day thach thuc phia truoc, con Iran mo cua mot so ga tau dien de nguoi dan co noi tru an khi co khong kich.

Nhung con so cho thay muc do leo thang

Theo thong tin duoc cap nhat trong bai, nhieu muc tieu tai Israel da bi tan cong bang ten lua va UAV, trong khi Tehran tiep tuc doi mat cac dot khong kich tu phia Israel. So luong thuong vong tang nhanh cho thay xung dot khong con dung o muc ran de, ma da chuyen sang mot chu ky dap tra nguy hiem hon.

Khi hai ben cung tang muc dap tra, bien so dang so nhat khong chi la so ten lua duoc phong di, ma la kha nang kiem soat nguong leo thang.

Tac dong vuot ra ngoai pham vi quan su

Dien bien nay khong chi gay suc ep len an ninh khu vuc ma con anh huong den thi truong dau, cuoc van tai va tam ly tai chinh toan cau. Cac quoc gia va doanh nghiep phu thuoc vao Trung Dong se tiep tuc theo doi sat, boi bat ky su co nao tren ban do an ninh deu co the lam gia tang chi phi va rui ro kinh doanh.
